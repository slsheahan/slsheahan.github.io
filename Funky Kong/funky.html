Funky Kong: A Discord Bot for Daily Music Posting

Funky Kong is a discord bot made for my friend group's server. Two of them are also programmers, so we have worked together to craft it to suit our specific needs.

The current focus of the bot is for our channel "VGM of the day", where a server member posts a piece of music from a video game. Since everyone on the server plays video games, there was a lot of push to be able to post. To keep it fair, we decided to implement a reservation system. When Google Sheets grew to be annoying, we had only one hope remaining: Funky Kong, the discord bot who was prophesized...
